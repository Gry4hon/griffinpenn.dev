College of Innovation and Design Event Space Designner



Tools used: Blender, Procreate, Adobe XD

This was another client project for Boise State University. I was apart of the modeling and design team, and my role involved drafting up concepts for the user interface and creating assets for the area designer. The web app was designed for the director of the college in order to design an event space for the second floor of the Albertsons Library on the Boise State Campus. This space is being remodeled during the summer of 2023 inorder to have an official place for innovators and other conferences will be held.
